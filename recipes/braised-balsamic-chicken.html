Braised Balsamic Chicken

Description

This chiken dish goes great with rice or pasta. Greenbeans are a perfect sid dish for a low carb option. This recipe makes 6 servings and takes 10 minutes to prep with a cooking time of 25 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1(14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup balsamic vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspon dried rosemary
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme

Steps

  1. Season both side of chicken breasts with garlic salt and pepper.
  2. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat; cook seasoned chicken breast until chicken is browned, 3 to 4 minutes.
  3. Pour diced tomatoes and balsamic vinegar over chicken; season with basil, oregano, rosemary, and thyme. Simmer until chicken is no longer pink and the juices run clear, about 15 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
